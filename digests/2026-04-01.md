**Power User Tips**
- **OpusPlan workflow** — Use `/model opus` for planning, then `Shift+Tab` to switch to Sonnet for execution. Keeps reasoning quality high while cutting token cost on the heavy lifting.
- **Hooks for audit trails** — The `PreToolUse` and `PermissionDenied` hooks (covered in Boris Cherny's recent tips) let you log every command Claude runs. Add to `settings.json` for a full audit trail on long agentic sessions.
- **Agent teams pattern** — Spin up 3+ parallel Claude Code agents on the same feature, then cherry-pick the best output. Especially effective for competing implementations where you're unsure of the right approach.
- **Let Claude verify its own work** — Pass it a definition of done (tests, lint rules, screenshots). Self-correction rates jump significantly compared to open-ended instructions.
